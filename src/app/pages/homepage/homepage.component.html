Chatham Evening School

The Chatham Evening School is a beacon of educational opportunity, catering specifically to individuals seeking to excel in their Caribbean Secondary Education Certificate (CSEC) and Caribbean Advanced Proficiency Examination (CAPE) studies. Offering a diverse range of courses, from core subjects to specialized electives, the school provides a flexible learning environment tailored to the needs of working adults and students balancing other commitments. With experienced educators and comprehensive resources, students are equipped with the knowledge and skills necessary to achieve academic success and pursue their aspirations with confidence. Whether aiming for academic advancement, career enhancement, or personal enrichment, the Chatham Evening School empowers learners to reach their full potential and unlock a world of opportunities.
